# Internal Linking (New Site)

This site is intentionally simple: **4 pages** designed to drive qualified calls.

## Primary goal
- Move a qualified visitor to the **Contact** page to request a Growth Plan.

## Pages (and what they do)
- **Homepage (`/`)**: Positioning + qualification + primary CTA
- **How It Works (`/how-it-works`)**: The process, clearly explained + CTA
- **About (`/about`)**: Credibility + operator-led positioning + CTA
- **Contact (`/contact`)**: Form submission

## Linking guidelines
- Keep CTAs consistent: **Get a Free Growth Plan**
- Link only between the four pages.
- Avoid linking to legacy paths (services, pricing, blog, city SEO pages). Those URLs are permanently redirected.
