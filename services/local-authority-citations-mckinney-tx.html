Why Consistency Matters

When your business name, address, and phone number match on your website, your Google Business Profile, and the directories and sites that mention you, Google has more confidence that you're a real, relevant local business. Inconsistencies—different spellings, old addresses, wrong phone numbers—can hurt your rankings and confuse customers. We help McKinney and North Texas businesses get listed correctly and build local authority through consistent citations and relevant links.
