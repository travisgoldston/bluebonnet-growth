Why is this different from the company that keeps calling me?

Because I live here, and my name is on the work. I’m not dialing hundreds of businesses from a script. I take a small number of clients, do the work myself with people I trust, and measure success the way you do: real leads, real jobs, real relationships—not numbers that only look good on paper.

Do I get a real person or a dashboard?

You get me. We’ll use tools when they help, but I’m not hiding behind software. If something breaks or feels off, you text or call.

What if I’m not tech-savvy?

That’s fine. I’ll explain choices in plain language—like a neighbor who happens to know this stuff cold. No lectures, no jargon wall.
