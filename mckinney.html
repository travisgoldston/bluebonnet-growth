McKinney is growing fast — and the competition shows.

McKinney has that rare mix of a historic downtown that keeps getting better and new growth that keeps spreading north and east. Between Highway 75 and the 380 corridor, you’ve got a wave of new neighborhoods and new businesses showing up right alongside the legacy companies that have been here for years.
