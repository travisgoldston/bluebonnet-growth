Local SEO for Melissa Small Businesses

If you run a good business in Melissa but feel invisible on Google, you’re not alone. I help nearby customers find you first, so more searches turn into calls.

Melissa is small, but it’s not sleepy anymore.

Melissa has been one of those “you either live here or you drive through it” towns for a long time. But along the 75 corridor, that’s changing fast. New subdivisions are bringing families, and families bring demand, for dentists, roofers, cleaners, lawn care, tutors, and everything else.

A lot of Melissa businesses used to survive on word of mouth, repeat customers, and being the name people remembered. Now the default is Google. When someone moves in from out of town, they’re not asking neighbors first. They’re searching.

And because people bounce between Melissa, Anna, McKinney, and Van Alstyne, your visibility needs to hold up across the nearby cities too, not just within one ZIP code.
