What Happens When You Reach Out

You fill out the form. We look at your business — your Google presence, your website, your reviews, your competition. Within a few days, we come back with a free Growth Plan: a plain-English breakdown of what's working, what's broken, and what to fix first.

The Bluebonnet Job Engine™

This is the system we use with every client. Three phases, each measured by one thing: more booked jobs.

Phase 1: Find the Leaks

Most home service companies are losing leads they don't even know about. Your Google Business Profile is half-filled. Your website ranks for your company name but not "emergency plumber in [your city]." Your competitor's site converts twice as many visitors because their phone number is actually visible on mobile.

We audit everything a potential customer touches: your Google listing, your website, your reviews, your local rankings. We score each one by how much revenue it's costing you — not by some arbitrary "marketing score."

You get: A prioritized list of exactly what's leaking leads, ranked by revenue impact. No jargon. No 80-page PDF you'll never read.

Phase 2: Build the Machine

This is where we fix what we found. We rebuild or optimize your site so it does one job: make the phone ring.

That means pages built around the services and cities that actually drive revenue for you. Headlines that say what you do, not marketing buzzwords. Calls to action that are impossible to miss. A Google Business Profile that's complete, accurate, and working for you in the map pack.

We don't redesign for the sake of redesigning. Every change ties back to a job on your calendar.

You get: A website and Google presence engineered to convert searchers into callers. Built around your highest-value services and areas.

Phase 3: Scale What Books

Once the foundation converts, we expand. New pages targeting your most profitable services in adjacent cities. Content that captures the long-tail searches your competitors aren't thinking about. More visibility where it matters — not everywhere.

We don't chase traffic numbers. We chase the searches that lead to $3,000, $5,000, $10,000+ jobs.

You get: Consistent, predictable growth in the calls and leads that actually turn into revenue.

We do one thing: build a system that gets your phone ringing with the right customers. Everything we do ladders up to that.
