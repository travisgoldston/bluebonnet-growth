Problem

Calendar-first budgeting wasn’t built for your paycheck

Monthly views break when your life runs on paydays. Rent, subscriptions, and groceries do not wait for the first of the month. If your app is built around a calendar grid, you are always translating — and that is where money leaks.

How it works

Three steps. Paycheck-native.

Budgetocity centers every plan on when cash actually hits your account, then rolls forward automatically.

1

Set your pay schedule

Weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, or irregular — tell us when you get paid and we build the rhythm.

2

Assign bills to pay periods

Drag rent to the paycheck that can cover it. Split larger goals across deposits without mental math.

3

Spend with clarity

See what is safe to spend until the next deposit. When life shifts, your plan adjusts — not your stress.
