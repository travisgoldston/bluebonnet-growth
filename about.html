About Bluebonnet

Bluebonnet exists for owners who want a straight answer. You get direct access—my phone number, text messages, and no “account manager” middle layer.

We care about work that shows up in the real world: conversations, booked jobs, and repeat customers—not busywork that looks good in a report.

If we work together, you should expect:

  • Plain language about what to fix and why
  • Local presence that matches how your neighbors search
  • A relationship where your reputation matters as much as mine

We only take a small number of clients at a time. If you’re shopping for the cheapest option, we’re probably not the right fit—and that’s okay.
