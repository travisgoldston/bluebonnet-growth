Built From the Trades

Bluebonnet Growth isn't a marketing agency that picked "home services" from a list of niches. We grew up in this world.

The Short Version

My name is Travis Goldston. My father is a master electrician — 30+ years in the field, calloused hands, the whole deal. I grew up watching him build something real. The kind of work where your reputation is your business card.

I went the digital route. Marketing, SEO, web design, conversion optimization. But I never lost sight of the businesses I grew up around — the ones that do excellent work but struggle to get found online.

What I kept seeing: good companies — the kind that show up on time, do the job right, and treat people well — getting outranked and outmarketed by competitors with bigger budgets and louder agencies. Not because those competitors were better. Because they were easier to find.

Bluebonnet Growth exists to fix that. I built the Bluebonnet Job Engine™ specifically for home service businesses because I understand the model. I know that a $7,000 HVAC install matters more than 10,000 website visitors. I know that a full Tuesday matters more than "brand awareness." And I know that most marketing companies have never been within ten feet of a service truck.

How We're Different

You work directly with me. No account managers. No junior team behind the curtain. When you text, I answer. When strategy changes, I'm the one making the call.

We measure what matters. Calls. Booked jobs. Revenue. Not impressions, not followers, not "engagement." If it doesn't show up on your calendar, it doesn't count.

We work with a small number of clients on purpose. I'd rather have 10 clients I can actually move the needle for than 50 I'm just "managing." Capacity is limited because attention is limited.

My Wife Victoria Runs With Me

Victoria handles operations, client communication, and keeps everything running while I'm deep in strategy and execution. When you work with Bluebonnet, you're working with both of us.

Why Home Services

Because my dad built his life in it. Because I grew up watching what it takes to run a trade business. And because the owners in this industry — the ones who are great at the work but invisible online — deserve better than what most marketing companies are selling them.

This isn't a niche we tested. It's the one we grew up in.
