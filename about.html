This is not an agency.

One operator. Five clients. Direct access. Built on real business experience.

I understand how businesses actually work.

Before I focused on local SEO, I spent nearly a decade inside real companies helping teams get better at the things that keep a business alive: customer service, sales, retention, scheduling, and operations.

I have sat in the meetings where payroll is tight and the phone is not ringing enough. I have seen what happens when a good crew does great work but nobody can find them on Google. I have fixed broken handoffs between the front desk and the field.

That background matters. SEO is not about rankings for their own sake. It is about filling your schedule with jobs from people who are already looking for what you do.

Why I do local SEO now.

I started Bluebonnet Growth as a side business on purpose. I want to help a small number of North Texas service companies get more visibility, calls, leads, and revenue through Google search. Not run a big agency. Not sell retainers for work that does not move the needle.

I work with pest control, HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electricians, landscapers, and similar trades. Businesses where the phone ringing is the whole game.

My job is simple: get you found on Google, get you more calls, and prove it with numbers you can tie to revenue. If I cannot do that, you should walk away.

What working with me looks like.

I take on 5 clients at a time. When you are one of them, you get my full attention.

You get my personal number. Text me when something feels off. You hear back from me, not a support ticket.

There are no long-term contracts. If the work is not paying for itself, stop. That is the deal.

I focus on three things: local SEO, Google Business Profile optimization, and SEO content. No branding packages. No social media menus. No strategy decks that never turn into calls.

If you want someone who has run real businesses and now helps service companies win on Google, apply below. If you want a big agency, I am not your person.
